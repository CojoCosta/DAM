Claudio Giráldez se busca un lío

Claudio Giráldez lined up Vicente Guaita in goal for Celta de Vigo this past Sunday. The Galician coach had already warned before the match about the goalkeeper and how he had been training normally with the rest of the group: "This is the first week that he really feels ready to compete. From there, we will decide between Iván and him. This week of break has been very good for him to be able to complete sessions with the group." With these statements, Guaita could intuit that his chances of playing against Valladolid were real, leaving, in this case, Iván Villar in goal.
